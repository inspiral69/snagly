# Snagly – AI Resale Assistant

Concept & design notes (from *Snagly_App_Concept_Notes.pdf*).

## Vision

Build an AI-powered app that identifies undervalued listings by comparing live marketplace listings with historical sold prices and estimating realistic resale profit. The aim is to surface only the highest-quality opportunities rather than overwhelming users with listings.

## Core User Journey

1. User creates a watch (e.g. "Sony 24-70 GM II").
2. AI analyses comparable sold items to understand typical value.
3. AI understands current listings (model, condition, accessories, faults, seller details).
4. Live monitoring checks for new listings.
5. When a profitable opportunity appears, the user receives a notification.
6. One tap opens the marketplace listing.

## AI Features

- Match comparable items intelligently rather than by keywords.
- Estimate fair resale value.
- Calculate expected profit after fees, shipping and other costs.
- Score each opportunity with a confidence rating.
- Explain WHY a listing is a good deal in plain English.

## Deal Screen

Display purchase price, estimated resale value, fees, shipping, estimated net profit, ROI, expected time to sell and AI confidence. Include a short AI explanation highlighting why the opportunity stands out.

## Portfolio

Track every purchase, resale price, fees and actual profit. Show monthly profit, ROI, average holding time and total flips.

## Future Roadmap

Expand beyond eBay to Facebook Marketplace, Vinted, Gumtree, Cash Converters, CEX and other marketplaces. Eventually become a universal AI-powered opportunity finder.

## Branding

**Working title:** Snagly.

Memorable, friendly, modern — evokes “snagging a bargain” without tying the app to one marketplace.

**Potential taglines:**

- AI finds the deals. You snag them.
- Snag smarter.
- Find it first.
- Never miss a bargain again.

## Design Direction

Minimal, premium feel with clean typography, generous spacing, and clear emphasis on confidence scores and estimated profit — more like a financial tool than a bargain-hunting app.
